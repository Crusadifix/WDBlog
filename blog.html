Blog Post 2: A Reflection on Assignment 2

04/23/2018

  • What was challenging about the assignment, and what specific kinds of problems did you have. How did you solve those problems?
    • I had difficulty matching my page to the images when using my workstation, as they were too low-resolution. I solved it by resizing things and changing zoom levels. That said, 4k images would be nice in the future.
  • What did you learn from the assignment? Were there any special insights you had? What did you find that you already knew?
    • I learned that perfectly remaking a design from an image is a pain in the neck. Also, I learned flex items do not interact with line breaks.
  • What kinds of resources were helpful for completing the assignment? Specific websites? Lectures? The class Piazza forum? The TAs? How did you use each of these resources?
    • www.w3schools.com was extremely useful. I used it to double check how to use properties I was less familiar with.
  • What are one or two things you had to Google to complete the assignment?
    • Setting a max number of flex elements displayed in a row.
